Who is Player Longevity Inc.?

We are leading sports scientists in our field. Our specialty is using data to give estimations of how long a player may play professionally. We use data from past players and run it through our patented SuperAlgorithm to provide teams with accurate data on a potential career length. In the world of 9 figure contracts, this could be very useful for any organization
